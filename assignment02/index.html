NEW YORK CITY

About NYC

As you know, New York is big city!

There is direct flights from Tokyo to New York everyday. It takes about 13 hours to New York by airplane.It's so far...

The time difference between Japan and New York is just 14 hours. When it's 8 a.m. in New York, it’s 10 p.m. in Japan.

New York has the lagest population in all prefectures of U.S.A. The population of New York is 8 million, Manhattan is around 1.6 million. About a hundred thousand Japanese live there.

New York made up of five areas. It's Manhattan, Brooklyn, The Bronx, Queens, and Staten Island.

There are many sightseeing spot in Manhattan. It’s the center of sightseeing spot. The Manhattan has a land area of about 59 square kilometers. It’s as large as Yamanote Line in Tokyo.

I visited there in October. It's cool and comfortable season.

New York is American's safest large city, but you shouldn't have lot of cash, when you go out. You always should have your stuff in your hands.
